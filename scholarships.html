College Admission Scholarship Application (CASA)

College Admission Scholarship Admission (CASA) is a conventional scholarship for Class 12th pass students that help to maximize the advantages of quality higher education for the students

Eligibility for CASA

Students who have passed the Class12th examination from CBSE, ICSE or any other recognized state board are eligible to apply.

Students who have finished graduation can also fill the form for post-graduation courses.

The institute must be AICTE or UGC recognized where the candidate goes for admission.

Schindler Igniting Minds Scholarship

Schindler Igniting Minds Scholarship is a big opportunity for scholars belonging to rural regions and underprivileged classes. The scholarship is especially for class 12th pass.he prime purpose of the Schindler scholarship is to grant financial support to worthy students. Each year, about 75 students are granted this scholarship, worth Rs. 20,000.

Eligibility for Schindler Igniting Minds Scholarship

Students who have qualified class 12th with science as a subject with a minimum of 65% aggregate.

The yearly family income of the student should not be more than Rs. 2 lakh

Applicants who are already persevering diploma courses can also apply for the scholarship.
